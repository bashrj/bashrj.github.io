Background

Marc Gebauer Lifestyle is a well-known luxury watch business based in Germany. With a reputation for excellence and a loyal customer base, the business is considered one of the leaders in the luxury watch market.

I was initially tasked by the Marc Gebauer team with the redesign of their product pages. The goal was to create a more user-friendly and visually appealing online view of their products.

I was later hired for many custom page developments to the storefront and to maintain the already existing ones

Product page
